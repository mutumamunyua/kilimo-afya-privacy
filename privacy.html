Privacy Policy for KILIMO AFYA

Last updated: August 5, 2025

KILIMO AFYA (“we”, “us”, “our”) built this app to help farmers diagnose crop diseases using AI. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Photos & videos: You upload images of your crops for disease detection.
  • Location: We use approximate and precise location for region-specific tips.
  • Login info: We support phone-number OTP and/or email login.
  • Diagnostics & analytics: Crash logs and performance data (to fix bugs).

How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Power AI-based disease analysis
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Authenticate and identify your account
  • Provide location-based treatment advice

Your Choices & Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing support@crophealthai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about your data, please email us at support@crophealthai.com.
